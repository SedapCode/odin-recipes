Maggot Cheese

Description

is a traditional Sardinian, Italy sheep milk cheese that contains live insect larvae (maggots). (source:wiki)

Ingredients

  • 1 wheel of Pecorino cheese

Steps

  1. Make a wheel of Pecorino cheese and place mature cheese wheel outdoors.
  2. Allow the Pioplhila casei, or cheese fly, to lay eggs in cheese.
